FAQ – Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new tournament?
To create a new tournament, go to the Create Tournament page and fill in the tournament name, dates, and organizer information. Then you can add divisions and teams.
How do I add a team?
Navigate to the Add Team page, enter the team name and player details, and click the save button. You can also manage existing teams from the tournament overview.
How do I set up match schedules?
Visit the Match Setup page to assign fields, choose match times, and generate a schedule based on the number of teams and matches.
How do I update match scores in real time?
Use the Scoreboard feature to update match scores live. Enter scores manually and they will be displayed in real time for both administrators and the audience.
How do I change tournament settings?
Go to the Settings page to adjust tournament configurations such as period duration, timeout length, and the number of periods.
